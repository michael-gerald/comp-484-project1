4 Albums I Like

Doo–Wops & Hooligans - Bruno Mars

Doo–Wops & Hooligans is Bruno Mars' debut studio album and it was released October 4, 2010. The album consists of 10 songs and is approximately 35 minutes long. Bruno Mars is definitely one of my favorite musical artists and I've been a fan of his since I was a kid. I remember my kindergarten class danced to Count on Me (this songs in this album) and 15 years later I'm still bumping my head listening to his songs. This albums a personal favorite of mine, and is probably my favorite one from his short discography. It's hard to say which of his albums is my favorite because all of them are good. But, I'll go with this one for now because of nostalgia factor. The album has this feels good vibe to it and even if I don't actively listen to Bruno Mars, I'll go back and listen to his songs. Some of my favorites from this album are The Lazy Song (because I'm super lazy), Talking to the Moon, and Just the Way You Are. All of the songs in this album are absolute bangers so I recommend listening to it.
