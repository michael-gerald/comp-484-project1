4 Albums I Like

Is This It – The Strokes

Is This It is the Strokes' debut studio album and it was released July 30, 2001. The album consists of 9 songs and is approximately 35 minutes long. This album probably isn't my favorite Strokes album (I like The New Abnormal more to be honest). But, when I thought about the albums I wanted to talk about, this one came to my mind first so I decided to do this one. Growing up, I would occasionally hear songs from The Strokes and just knew of their existence. Around 2 to 3 years ago, I decided to listen to this album. My thoughts back then when I first listened to this album and when I listened to this album recently are the same — it's a good album and I like it. In terms of the meaning of the song, I think the whole album expresses something along the lines of Is this all life has to offer?. Some of my favorites in this album are Someday and Hard To Explain.
