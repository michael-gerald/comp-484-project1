4 Albums I Like

The Stranger – Billy Joel

The Stanger is Billy Joel's fifth studio album and it was released on September 29, 1977. The album consists of 9 songs and is approximately 42 minutes long. This is probably one of my favorite albums I've listened to (maybe like top 6 or 7). Growing up, I remember I would hear Billy Joel songs in my dad's car and until I listened to this album, I would think of Billy Joel as the Piano Man guy. This is one of those albums that I could always go back to, and I definitely have the past couple of years. Some of my favorites are Scenes from an Italian Restaurant, Vienna, and Just the Way You Are.
